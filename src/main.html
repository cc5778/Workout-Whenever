Why WorkoutWhenever?

  • WorkoutWhenever is a website for all users, regardless of skill, experience, or ability.
  • Through WorkoutWhenever, users have access to specialized workout that take in preferences such as choice Pilates, Calisthenics, or Weighted workouts as well as the choice of workouts that are possible for those with disabilities.
  • Users have access to these workouts whenever needed as long as they are signed in for the sake of saving their preferences and workouts based on those preferances.

About Us

  1. The team of WorkoutWhenever is a pool of passionate, curious, and highly taleted indiviuals who come together to create specialized workouts for every user.
  2. We aim to empower our users for the sake of the power that comes with testing the limits of thier bodies in a healthy way.
  3. We also seek to make this an equitable tool, so all that is required of users is their preferences for specialized workouts and an account for workouts to be saved.
